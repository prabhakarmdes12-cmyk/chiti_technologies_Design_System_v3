III.A — The Chiti Ethical Manifesto

Our system governs human behavior at scale. Every component must adhere to the following proprietary laws before deployment.

The Frictionless Commerce Pledge

No scarcity countdown timers. No hidden subscription checkboxes. No dark-pattern exit flows. All commerce components must generate un-coerced intent.

Algorithmic Transparency Doctrine

Any conversational AI surface (Project Nova) must render a visible SYSTEM_ID flag. Users must know they are interacting with an AI at all times.
